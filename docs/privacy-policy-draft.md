# Privacy policy draft (not legal advice — Legal must finalize)

**Product:** Cyber Guardian AI  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-10

## What we process

- Account email (if you register)
- URL / file hashes you submit for scanning
- Consent records and emergency dry-run logs
- Optional anonymized risk metadata

## What we do **not** do

- Upload raw SMS / chat contents to the cloud
- Sell personal data
- Attack third-party systems

## Emergency reporting

If enabled with prior written consent, Critical incidents may generate an
**anonymized** report. Live delivery to authorities requires AQ-039 allowlists
and remains dry-run by default.
